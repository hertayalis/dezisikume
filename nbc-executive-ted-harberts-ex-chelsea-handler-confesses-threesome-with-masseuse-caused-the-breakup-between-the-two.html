NBC Executive Ted Harbert's Ex Chelsea Handler Confesses Threesome with Masseuse Caused the Breakup

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 29, 2024

Renowned comedian and former NBC executive Ted Harbert’s ex-girlfriend, Chelsea Handler, recently shared a surprising revelation during an interview with Andy Cohen on SiriusXM. Handler admitted to engaging in a secret tryst with an unnamed female masseuse, which ultimately led to the demise of her relationship with Harbert. +

Chelsea Handler’s Threesome Revelation

During her conversation with Andy Cohen, Handler candidly responded to a question about her dating history with women. She clarified that while she had not dated a woman, she had engaged in a sexual encounter with one. The 48-year-old stand-up star went on to reveal that she had a series of encounters with a massage therapist, without Harbert’s knowledge.

“I was very turned on by this woman,” Handler confessed to Cohen.

She described the affair as a catalyst for a major realization about her relationship with Harbert. As she became more interested in the masseuse, Handler knew it was time to end her relationship with Harbert.

Impact on the Relationship

Handler admitted that she never informed Harbert about her involvement with the masseuse, and jokingly added, “I mean, hopefully, he’s listening now.”

Harbert was reportedly in Portugal at the time, working on a house with his wife.

The comedian’s revelation sheds light on the underlying issues in her relationship with Harbert. Looking back, Handler reflected on her experience, revealing that her disinterest in watching her own show after work was a contributing factor to their breakup.

“That was the reason it didn’t work out, ultimately. I think,” she disclosed in a 2012 interview with Marie Claire.

Handler’s Views on Threesomes

The topic of threesomes continued during the interview, as Cohen asked Handler if she would ever consider gifting her partner with a threesome.

Handler swiftly responded, “Absolutely. It’s fun. Absolutely.”

However, she made it clear that she preferred threesomes involving another woman, expressing her lack of interest in engaging in a threesome with two men.

“I mean, I don’t want to get double-teamed by a guy,” Handler elaborated, “but I like another girl in the situation. You know, she can do all the stuff that I’m too lazy to do.”

Conclusion

Chelsea Handler’s revelation about her secret tryst with a masseuse highlights the impact it had on her relationship with Ted Harbert. The realization that she was more interested in the masseuse ultimately led to their breakup. Handler’s candidness regarding her preferences and views on threesomes adds another layer to her unique perspective on relationships. Despite the end of her relationship with Harbert, Handler continues to navigate the world of love and dating, having most recently ended her one-year relationship with comedian Jo Koy.
