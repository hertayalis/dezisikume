Zoey Stark Turns On Nikkita Lyons In Surprising Fashion On WWE NXT

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 14, 2024

WWE’s developmental brand continues bringing the best action they can to the USA Network on a weekly basis. This week, a big title match capped off the white and gold brand’s show, and it was a big night for Nikkita Lyons and Zoey Stark.

After a long road, Zoey Stark and Nikkita Lyons finally faced Katana Chance and Kayden Carter for the NXT Women’s Tag Team Titles. That match did not end as the babyfaces planned.

Chance and Carter retained their titles, and then both teams met in the middle of the ring. As they showed respect to each other after a hard-fought match, Zoey Stark did the unthinkable.

Stark snatched one of the WWE Women’s Tag Team Titles, and she smashed it over Nikkita Lyon’s head. This was a brutal shot and a total surprise.

Chance and Carter ran out of the ring to avoid taking a shot from Stark as well. Needless to say, this was her braking point, and now Stark vs Lyons looks to be on the agenda.

We’ll have to see what happens next, but it doesn’t appear that Katana Chance or Kayden Carter will be dropping their WWE Women’s Tag Team Titles any time soon.
