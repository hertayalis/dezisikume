Becky Lynch the First Woman to Qualify for 2024 Elimination Chamber Match

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, August 1, 2024

WWE’s next Premium Live Event is set to take place in Perth Australia later this month. Hence, WWE is already looking to build towards this PLE.

The Elimination Chamber PLE will be headlined by two Elimination Chamber matches, one for the and one for the women. It was recently announced that there would be a women’s Elimination Chamber match at the PLE where the winner would go on to face Rhea Ripley at WrestleMania 40.

Hence, over the next few weeks, WWE will be hosting a series of qualifying matches to determine the participants of the Elimination Chamber match. The first qualifying match took place tonight on WWE RAW between Becky Lynch and Shayna Baszler.

As expected, both Becky Lynch and Shayna Baszler brought the fight to each other during the match. Baszler came close to beating Lynch a couple of times. However, The Man proved why she is the best female wrestlers by beating Baszler after hitting the Manhandle slam.

With this win, Becky Lynch gets one step closer to challenging Rhea Ripley at WrestleMania 40 but she will have a gargantuan task ahead of her at Elimination Chamber. Zoey Stark and Liv Morgan will face each other in another Elimination Chamber qualifying match next week. Stay tuned to Ringside News as we bring you live coverage of WWE RAW.
