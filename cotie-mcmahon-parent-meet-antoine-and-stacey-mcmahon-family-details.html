Cotie McMahon Parent | Meet Antoine And Stacey McMahon: Family Details

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, May 31, 2024

Cotie McMahon is a young college basketball star. She attributes her extraordinary achievement to her parents’ unwavering encouragement and influence.

McMahon rose to popularity after receiving the Big Ten Conference Women’s Basketball Freshman of the Year title in 2023. Her parents have been huge supporters and influencers throughout her career. Cotie McMahon is an American college basketball player who represents the Ohio State Buckeyes in the Big Ten Conference.

She dominated the basketball court, leading her team to the Greater Western Ohio Conference championship and earning a four-star rating from ESPN. McMahon, who entered at Ohio State at the age of 17, made an immediate impact as a rookie, garnering second-team All-Big Ten honours and the coveted Big Ten Rookie of the Year title.

In her debut NCAA tournament, she led Ohio State to the Elite Eight, leaving an indelible impression as a dynamic player with a penchant for big circumstances. McMahon went on to win gold medals at the 2022 FIBA Under-18 Women’s Americas Championship and the 2023 FIBA Under-19 World Cup, with noteworthy performances including a game-winning shot in the final.

Meet Cotie McMahon’s Parents

Cotie McMahon was born on May 4, 2004, to loving and caring parents, Antoine and Stacey McMahon. Her parents had a huge influence on her early determination to seek a successful sports career. Cotie’s father, Antoine McMahon, has athletic experience, having played football at Wayne High School in Ohio. He has been a positive influence in her life and basketball career.

Speaking about her father, Cotie stated,

“He is always there for me. He’s always motivated me to do better. He’s always told me that I can accomplish everything I set my mind to.

Moving on, McMahon’s mother, Stacey, introduced her to basketball when she was younger. Stacey has played a vital role in encouraging and advancing Cotie’s basketball career. Cotie revealed that she was initially indifferent in basketball until her mother pushed her to try it. McMahon stated that her mother was the one who took her to the gym and rehearsed for hours.

During an interview, she explained:

“She always pushed me to work harder. She was constantly forcing me to play against boys or older girls. She was continuously having me do more exercises or shots.”

Cotie McMahon is fortunate to have such supportive parents. She also frequently shows gratitude and honours her parents for her accomplishments.

Cotie McMahon Siblings

Cotie McMahon grew up with four siblings. She has two brothers, Bryce and Liam McMahon, and two sisters, Joss and Harley McMahon. All of Cotie’s siblings encouraged and supported her in her career. They all have strong relationships with everyone.

Furthermore, all of McMahon’s siblings are involved in sports, demonstrating that the family has a strong athletic heritage. Clearly, her family’s support and shared love of sports strengthens the McMahon family bond. Furthermore, it adds incentive to Cote’s course.
