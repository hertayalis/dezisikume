What is the difference between residential and commercial water heaters?

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, July 26, 2024
Spread the love

Answer

According to Reliant Energy, a Texas-based energy company, there are two significant variations between commercial and domestic water heaters: the size of the storage tanks and the amount of gas used (for gas-fired heaters). Residential heater tanks are normally no more than 100 gallons in capacity, whereas commercial heater tanks are no more than 250 gallons.
