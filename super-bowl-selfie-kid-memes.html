Funniest Super Bowl Selfie Kid Memes

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Justin Timberlake unfortunately didn't include any guest performers in his Super Bowl halftime show last night, but his set did bring forth an unexpected internet star: a boy now known as Selfie Kid. When Timberlake went into the audience for the end of his performance, a young fan caught everyone's attention when he danced along and tried to get a selfie with the pop star.

The 13-year-old Minnesota native reacted to becoming a viral phenomenon after the game. "I got to be on TV with Justin Timberlake," he told ABC News. "Everyone's texting me and stuff. My phone's going crazy!"

He explained how he ended up next to JT on live television. "He was coming. My dad told me," he added. "And I just like jumped in there and it was just crazy."

Congrats to the Eagles and everything, but let's be honest—this kid is the real winner of the Super Bowl.
