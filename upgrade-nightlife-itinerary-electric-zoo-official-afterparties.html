Upgrade Your Nightlife Itinerary With Electric Zoos Official Afterparties

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, May 23, 2024

Recommended Videos

Half the fun of hitting any major festival is seeing the surrounding area transform into an EDM hotspot for a number of days. New York City already boasts several decades of electronic music history, but the list of official afterparties for Electric Zoo prove Labor Day Weekend an ideal time to party in the city that never sleeps.

The festivities kick off on Thursday with an official pre-party at Lavo headlined by Pierce Fulton. The following three days – during which the festival itself takes place – features acts like 3LAU, Claude VonStroke and Markus Shulz across Pacha, Output, Webster Hall and Marquee. Most notably, that Saturday a surprise headliner will grace the decks at Webster Hall.

Take a look at the list of official afterparties below, and check back for more news on Electric Zoo as the dates of the festival approach.
