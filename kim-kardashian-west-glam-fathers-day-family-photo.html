Kim Kardashian West is no stranger to curating a high-fashion family photo.

On Sunday, the reality star took to Instagram to share a new image of her entire family in honor of Father's Day. Kim and her husband, rapper Kanye West, can be seen posing alongside their four children, North, Saint, Chicago and Psalm West, with the scenic Wyoming landscape as their backdrop. The family have been vacationing in Wyoming since celebrating North's seventh birthday last week.

In the ultra-glam image, the entire family are wearing custom outfits by Black-owned brand Mowalola, with North in a neon cow-print shift dress, Kim and Kanye in similar Matrix-esque leather coats, and Saint and Psalm matching in royal-blue jumpsuits. In another snap, West's father, Ray West, joins in for the photo op, casually dressed in a T-shirt, jeans, and a baseball cap.

"Happy Fathers Day to the best dad!!!! Thank you for making life so much fun! You give our kids the most awesome life! I love you so much!!!" Kim wrote alongside the family pic.
