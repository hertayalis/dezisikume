Andrew Walker Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, June 16, 2024

Who is Andrew Walker?

Andrew Walker is a Canadian actor and film producer who came into the limelight after appearing as Michael “Mike” Downey in the 2006 film, Steel Toes.

He is also known for appearing in the movies like Hot Properties, Against the Wall, Maybe It’s Me, and many others.

He subsequently appeared in films such as The Mountie and The Gundown and had a starring role in the Lifetime cop drama.

When did Andrew Walker born?

Andrew Walker was born in Montreal, Quebec, Canada on June 9, 1979. His birth name is Andrew William Walker and he is currently 42 years old.

His father’s name is Bruce Walker (a school administrator) and his mother’s name is Joyce Walker (a librarian).

He has a sibling namely, Jenimay Walker, an actress as well as a model. Andrew holds Canadian citizenship, but his ethnicity is unknown. His birth sign is Gemini.

Education, School/ College University

Talking about Andrew’s education history, Walker completed his graduation from Vanier College. He played volleyball for the college team. Walker further received a full scholarship to play volleyball for Boston College.

Andrew Walker: Professional Life, Career

Talking about his profession, Andrew started his acting career in Montreal as a recurring lead on the series Student Bodies in 1996-1997, a series lead on Back to Sherwood, and then Radio Active 1998-2000.

Also, after a month after Andrew Walker arrived in Hollywood he booked a series lead on the TV show Maybe It’s Me, then Sabrina, the Teenage Witch. He then appeared in numerous television series including, Maybe It’s Me Sabrina the Teenage Witch, Hot Properties, and many others.

Andrew Walker also appeared in the 2007 Lifetime film Abducted: Fugitive for Love portraying the charismatic Jack Carlson, which was a huge hit among the female demographic.

Subsequently, Walker acquired a large fan base. Likely, he went on to guest star on ER, CSI: Miami, CSI: NY, Without a Trace, Reba, and The Big Bang Theory.

In 2012, he starred in his first Hallmark Channel movie A Bride for Christmas alongside Arielle Kebbel. He also starred in his next Hallmark movie, “A Dream of Christmas” with Nikki DeLoach in 2016.

Likewise, in 2017, Andrew Walker starred in six more Hallmark feature films: “Love Struck Cafe,” “Dashing Through the Snow,” “Appetite for Love,” “Date With Love,” and “Love on Ice,” and as well as in “Bridal Wave.” In 2018, he starred in another Hallmark feature film, “Love in Design.”

Awards, Nomination

Andrew Walker was nominated for Outstanding Performance – Male for Snowed-Inn Christmas (2017) at ACTRA Montreal Award. Likewise, he won Best Actor for Steel Toes (2007) at Phillip Borsos Award.

Andrew Walker: Net Worth, Income, Salary

Andrew Walker has an estimated net worth of around $2 million as of 2022, earned from his professional career.

Andrew is the co-founder of Little West, a Food & Beverage brand.

Rumors, Controversy/ Scandal
